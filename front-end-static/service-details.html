Lab Two Project Focus

Our main focus for Lab Two was to implement a functional deep learning model that can take a user-uploaded image from a smartphone and accurately classify it.

How DermaScan Analyzes Your Skin

When you upload a photo to DermaScan, it goes through a multi-step pipeline. First, the image is cropped and normalized to ensure our model only focuses on the lesion itself, removing background noise like hair or uneven lighting.

  • Trained on thousands of dermoscopic images (HAM10000 dataset).
  • Utilizes advanced CNN architectures (e.g., ResNet, EfficientNet).
  • Provides a probability score indicating the likelihood of malignancy.

We've heavily relied on open-source libraries and frameworks such as TensorFlow and PyTorch to build and train our models. Our team spent weeks fine-tuning hyperparameters to reduce false negatives, which is crucial in medical applications.

It is important to remember that while our AI is highly capable, it is not infallible. A biopsy and examination by a certified dermatologist remain the gold standard for diagnosing melanoma. DermaScan is designed to be the first step in your journey to better skin health.
